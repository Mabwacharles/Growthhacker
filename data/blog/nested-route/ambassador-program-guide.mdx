### What is an Ambassador Program

A brand ambassador program is a marketing strategy where a company/organization recruits, mobilizes and empowers individuals to promote their products and services to a diverse network with the goal of achieving specific marketing goals, that may range frorm increasing product adoption, increasing sales, driving conversions, increasing customer loyalty and retention, creating trust and credibility and building brand awareness.

An ambassador will aggressively promote your product to their social networks in order to reach the program's objectives. This advocacy might take place two strategies either online or offline event representation for offline activations this may be in form of (events, conferences, workshops, trade exhibits) or online (blog articles, social media accounts). Typically, both are present.

### Who are brand ambassadors

Brand ambassadors are simply brand evangelists who are eager to promote your company to customers and gush about its products and services. They are typically chosen from your ideal target market and audience and don't require much persuasion because they are already admirers of you and what you do.

### How Ambassador programs work

Ambassador programs involve:

<ol>
  <li>Getting early adopters, users and fans who have influence among your target audience.</li>
  <li>
    Onboarding these users to your platform to be your representatives in pushing your brand
    awareness and adoption among other customers and users
  </li>
  <li>
    Developing a comprehensive educational initiatives to enhance the ambassadors understanding and
    proficiency in utilizing your product
  </li>
  <li>Set forth metrics and key perfomance indicators to track the ambassadors progress</li>
  <li>Reward the ambassadors for their efforts. </li>
  <li>Track the progress of your brand ambassador program.</li>
</ol>
